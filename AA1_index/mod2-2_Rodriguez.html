Assassination Classroom

An unknown creature appeared on Earth claiming to obliterate everything in a year while destroying 70% of the moon. The government tasked Class 3-E, which consisted of failing pupils, the opportunity to eliminate it within a year for a prize of $10 billion. However, given that the creature can move at a speed of Mach 20, this appeared impossible.
